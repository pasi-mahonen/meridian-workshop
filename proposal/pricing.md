| Role | Days | Day Rate | Cost |

| Tech Lead | 20 days | $1,000 | $20,000 |
| Frontend Developer | 20 days | $1,000 | $20,000 |

## Payment Terms

We propose a milestone-based payment schedule:

| Milestone | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Contract execution | $18,000 (30%) |
| Architecture doc + Reports remediation complete (end Week 3) | $18,000 (30%) |
| Restocking view complete (end Week 5) | $12,000 (20%) |

## Assumptions

Pricing is based on the following assumptions. Material changes to any of these may require a scope adjustment.

- Work is performed remotely; no travel costs are included
- Meridian provides access to the repository and a test environment within 3 business days of contract execution
- No third-party software licenses, infrastructure, or database migration are required
- Desired items (D1–D3) are confirmed or declined before Week 6
